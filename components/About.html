Institute of Business Management

The Institute of Business Management (IoBM) is committed to cater to the demands of the evolving challenges of learning and teaching by enabling and leveraging technology in the pursuit of scholarship. Insightful as well as relevant research is undertaken that creates economic and societal impact.

IoBM tutors innovative mindsets by providing a supportive environment to nurture entrepreneurship and intrapreneurship. IoBM aims to foster the ability of critical thinking through experiential learning, inquiry based learning and case teaching across several dimensions. Aiming to prepare for the challenges of inclusive growth and sustainability, it advocates the development of future leaders to meet the economic challenges emanating from the evolving local and global paradigms.

College of Computer Science & Information Systems

The College of Computer Science and Information Systems (CCSIS) at the Institute of Business Management (IoBM) is dedicated to advancing education and research in computing and analytics. It aims to empower learners with technological and analytical tools, fostering innovation and societal impact.

CCSIS offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including BS Computer Science, BS Data Science, MS Computer Science, and PhD in Computer Science. These programs are designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge, critical thinking, and ethical practices to address complex computing challenges.

The college emphasizes experiential learning through industry collaborations, workshops, and seminars. For instance, CCSIS organized an industrial visit to Systems Limited, providing students with insights into the IT industry's dynamics. Additionally, CCSIS hosted a Final Year Project showcase, highlighting innovative student projects.

CCSIS's mission aligns with IoBM's vision of fostering inclusive growth and sustainability by developing future leaders equipped to meet evolving technological challenges.

IEEE IoBM Student Branch

At IEEE IoBM Student Branch , we don’t just follow technology we shape its future. As a thriving hub of innovators, problem-solvers, and tech enthusiasts, our student branch serves as a bridge between academia and the ever-evolving world of technology. We are part of the world’s largest technical organization, empowering students at the Institute of Business Management (IoBM) with the knowledge, skills, and industry exposure needed to excel in the fast-paced world of science, engineering, and technology. Through cutting-edge workshops, insightful seminars, hands-on projects, and competitive hackathons, IEEE IOBM creates opportunities for students to engage with industry leaders, explore emerging trends, and develop real-world solutions. Our goal is to ignite curiosity, foster innovation, and equip future leaders with the expertise to drive meaningful change. IEEE IOBM is more than just a society it’s a launchpad for the next generation of tech pioneers, visionaries, and changemakers who are ready to challenge the status quo, redefine possibilities, and transform the world one innovation at a time.

Technova

TechNova is a premier tech and innovation event bringing together students, professionals, and enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds. With a unique blend of technical and non-technical modules, the event provides an engaging platform for participants to showcase their skills, compete in exciting challenges, and explore emerging technologies.

Beyond competitions, TechNova fosters a vibrant atmosphere with an opening ceremony, a lively social event, and a formal dinner, creating ample opportunities for networking and collaboration. The event concludes with a grand prize distribution and closing ceremony, recognizing the outstanding achievements of participants.

Open to university, college, and private students, TechNova is more than just a competition it’s an immersive experience designed to inspire innovation, celebrate talent, and build a thriving tech community.
