Maths Mania

Maths Mania is an exciting and intellectually stimulating competition designed to challenge students’ problem-solving abilities and analytical thinking. It offers a dynamic platform where participants tackle a variety of mathematical puzzles, logic-based problems, and rapid-fire questions. The competition aims to promote a love for mathematics through engaging and competitive activities that go beyond traditional classroom learning. Whether it’s algebra, geometry, number theory, or mental math, Maths Mania tests speed, accuracy, and reasoning under pressure. Ideal for students who enjoy challenges, this event fosters teamwork, sharpens critical thinking, and encourages creative approaches to solving mathematical problems in a fun and rewarding environment.
